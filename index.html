Our Story

Everyone knows that human beings are not symmetrical; sometimes we have two slightly different eyes, or two nostrils of different sizes and it is possible and frequent that our breasts are also asymmetrical. Moreover, in 2020 for example, more than 2.2 million cases of breast cancer have been reported (which makes it the most common cancer) and most of the time, to treat this cancer, a part or the whole affected breast is removed. I decided to create this brand for these two reasons. Most of the time, bras are not adapted to our morphology. My team and I want to make this undergarment, often described as uncomfortable, enjoyable to wear.
